Apple – Internal Apps

Lead designer improving and extending the internal application experience for Apple's teams.

Project Overview

As the lead designer for Apple's internal tools, I was tasked with improving and extending the application experience for various teams within the company. The project aimed to streamline workflows, increase productivity, and enhance user satisfaction across multiple internal platforms.

Key Challenges

  • Unifying disparate systems and interfaces across different departments
  • Balancing complex functionality with intuitive design for a diverse user base
  • Ensuring scalability to accommodate future growth and new features
  • Maintaining consistency with Apple's design language while optimizing for internal use

Solution & Approach

Our approach involved a comprehensive strategy combining user research, iterative design processes, and close collaboration with development teams:

  • Conducted extensive user interviews and workflow analysis across different departments
  • Developed a unified design system tailored for internal applications
  • Created high-fidelity prototypes and conducted usability testing
  • Implemented a modular design approach to ensure scalability and consistency

Impact & Results

The redesigned internal apps significantly improved efficiency and user satisfaction across Apple's teams:

  • Reduced onboarding time for new employees by 40%
  • Increased overall productivity by 25% across targeted departments
  • Improved user satisfaction scores by 35%
  • Established a scalable design system for future internal applications
